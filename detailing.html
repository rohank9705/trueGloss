Paint Correction & Detailing

Premium care to bring that showroom gloss back to your vehicle. A paint correction process involves a complete decontamination of the vehicle's paintwork, followed by a machine polish across all the panels to eliminate scratches and swirls embedded deep in the paint. To protect the paintwork from further damage, we recommend a ceramic coating application to seal in the gloss and create a hydrophobic layer that will last up to 7 years.

Detailing & Correction FAQ

Common questions about paint correction and ceramic coatings.

Valeting is the thorough cleaning of your vehicle's interior and exterior. Detailing goes a step further, focusing on restoration—such as machine polishing to remove scratches (paint correction) and applying long-term protection like ceramic coatings.

Our machine polishing process permanently removes swirl marks, holograms, and light-to-medium scratches. As a general rule, if you can run your fingernail across a scratch and it catches, it is too deep to safely remove completely, but its appearance will still be massively reduced.

Depending on the specific grade of ceramic coating you choose and how well the vehicle is maintained afterwards, our coatings offer durable, high-gloss protection for up to 7 years.
